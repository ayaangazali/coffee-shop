Our Story

The Daily Grind started back in 2012 as a tiny cart on the corner of Bean Street. What began as a one man operation with a single espresso machine has grown into the neighborhood coffee shop you know today.

We roast all of our beans in small batches and we believe a good cup of coffee can make anyone's day a little better. Every drink is made by hand by people who actually care.

Stop by and say hi. We saved you a seat.
